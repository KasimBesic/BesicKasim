Our Classes

Check out our classes suitable for all levels:

  • Beginner's Boxing: Perfect for those new to boxing, this class focuses on fundamental techniques, footwork, and basic combinations. It's a great starting point for anyone interested in learning the sport.
  • Olympic Boxing: Designed for intermediate to advanced boxers, this class emphasizes advanced techniques, tactics, and strategies inspired by Olympic-style boxing. It's suitable for those looking to take their skills to the next level and potentially compete.
  • Cardio Boxing: A high-energy class combining boxing drills with cardiovascular exercises. It's a fantastic way to improve overall fitness, burn calories, and relieve stress while learning basic boxing moves. No prior boxing experience required!
